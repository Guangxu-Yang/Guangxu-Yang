Block-sensitivity to Communication Lifting Theorem

(with Jiapeng Zhang)

Lifting theorems are a generic way to lift hardness of boolean functions to lower bounds in communication complexity, with many applications in different areas, such as proof complexity, game theory, combinatorial optimization.The lifting theorem for the block-sensitivity has been well-studied in previous papers via reduction [Zhang09][GP14] or information complexity approach [HN12]. In this paper, we prove a lifting theorem for the block-sensitivity by path simulation. We show that \(\mathbf{P}^{\mathrm{cc}}(f\circ g^n) = \Omega(q\cdot \mathrm{bs}(f))\) for any for all gadgets \(g:\{0,1\}^q\times \{0,1\}^q \rightarrow \{0,1\}\) with \(q > 2\) that have exponentially-small discrepancy.We also provide an alternate proof of the optimal lower bound of \(\Omega(n)\) for the deterministic communication complexity of the Tribes function by our path simulation technique and the technique might benefit further research.

A Decision Tree Based Analysis of communication complexity for FORK Game(Not Intended to Publish)

In their seminal paper, [GS91] introduce the FORK game to prove monotone depth lower bound for s-t connectivity. Due to the research of lifting theorem for equality gadget [LM18][dRMN+20] and direct sum of relations [Mei18], FORK game has received more attention in recent years. In this paper, we present a new proof that gives an decision tree interpretation of previous proof by path simulation technique and proving a disperser property of equality gadget. The new proof gives a new perspective on previous proof and lifting theorem for equality gadget.

Lifting Expander Graphs via Gap Amplification (Work in Progress)

(with Jiapeng Zhang)

The deterministic communication complexity of Tseitin search problem imply monotone depth complexity of an monotone function in \(\sf NP\). [GP14] prove a \(\Omega(n/\log n)\) lower bound via critical block sensitivity and [PR17] prove a \(\Omega(n)\) lower bound via degree to rank lifting theorem. In this paper, we prove a \(\Omega(n)\) deterministic communication lower bound of Tseitin search problem via path simulation.
